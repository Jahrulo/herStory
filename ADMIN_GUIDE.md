# Admin Guide for Embracing HERstory Website

## How to Access Your Admin Panel

There are **two easy ways** to access the blog management panel:

### Option 1: Click "Site Admin" Link (Easiest!)
1. Scroll down to the **bottom of any page** on your website
2. Look for the small **"Site Admin"** link in the footer (bottom right corner)
3. Click it to go to the login page

### Option 2: Direct URL
- Go to: **https://herstory.onrender.com/admin/login**
- Or just type `/admin/login` after your website URL

## Logging In

## Writing a Blog Post

1. Click **"Create New Post"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**: The headline of your post
   - **Theme**: Category (e.g., "Feminism", "Law & Justice")
   - **Excerpt**: A short summary (2-3 sentences)
   - **Content**: Your full blog post (supports rich text formatting)
   - **Author**: Your name (pre-filled)
   - **Date**: When you want it published
3. Click **"Create Post"**
